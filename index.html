About EPMCH

About East Point Medical College & Hospital

An NMC-approved medical college in Bangalore, run by a charitable trust, offering undergraduate and postgraduate medical education with attached hospital training.

🏆 NMC-Approved · RGUHS-Affiliated Medical College

East Point Medical College & Hospital (EPMCH) — also known as East Point College of Medical Sciences & Research Centre — is a private medical college in Bangalore, Karnataka, run by a charitable trust and offering MBBS, MD and MS programmes. The college is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS) and approved by the National Medical Commission (NMC), and combines classroom learning with clinical training through its attached teaching hospital.

The college sits on a large campus on the eastern side of Bangalore and includes an on-campus hospital, hostel blocks for men and women, laboratories, lecture halls and an auditorium. Exact fee structures, current bed strength and seat-wise cutoffs are revised by the institution and counselling authorities each year — Apex Learning Network can confirm the latest verified figures for your specific category before you apply.

MBBS Admission

MBBS Admission 2026 — East Point Medical College

NMC-approved MBBS programme. NEET UG is mandatory for every category, including management quota.

🚨 MBBS admissions open for 2026–27 · NEET UG required for all categories · Confirm current seat availability with Apex Learning Network

MBBS Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English — minimum percentage as per NMC/KEA norms (relaxed for SC/ST/OBC)
  • Minimum age 17 years by the admission year's cutoff date
  • NEET UG qualifying score — mandatory for every quota, with no exceptions
  • Registration for KEA counselling (for state-quota and most management-quota seats)
  • Valid documents: 10th & 12th mark sheets, NEET UG scorecard, Aadhaar, category certificate (if applicable)
Step 1–2 · Qualify NEET UG & check your result

Register for and appear in NEET UG, conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). Your NEET UG rank and score determine eligibility and the colleges you can realistically target — qualifying cutoffs are announced by NTA each year and vary by category.

Step 3 · Register for KEA counselling

Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) conducts centralised counselling for government and a portion of private-quota MBBS seats. Registration requires your NEET scorecard, academic certificates and ID proof. Confirm the current year's registration window on the official KEA website.

Step 4 · Document verification

Carry original mark sheets, NEET scorecard, Aadhaar, transfer/migration certificates and category certificates (if applicable) to the designated KEA verification centre.

Step 5 · Choice filling

List East Point Medical College among your preferences on the KEA portal in order of priority. Apex Learning Network can help you sequence preferences sensibly based on your rank.

Step 6 · Seat allotment

Seats are allotted across multiple rounds (including mop-up and stray vacancy rounds) based on NEET UG rank and choice filling. Check the KEA portal directly for your allotment status each round.

Step 7 · Fee payment

Once allotted a seat, confirm the official fee amount and payment deadline directly with the college admission office before transferring any funds. Apex Learning Network can verify this on your behalf.

Step 8 · Admission confirmation

Submit your allotment letter, NEET scorecard, academic certificates and required medical/character certificates at the college admission office to complete enrolment.

⚠️ Avoid unofficial agents promising "guaranteed seats" without NEET qualification — this is not how MBBS admission works for any quota in Karnataka. Apply only through verified counselling channels or directly with the college, and treat any consultancy (including ours) as a guide, not a gatekeeper to the seat itself.
